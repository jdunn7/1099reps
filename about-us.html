About 1099 REPS

Connecting independent medical sales representatives with healthcare companies since 2020

Our Story

1099 REPS was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between talented independent medical sales representatives and healthcare companies seeking top sales talent.

After years in the medical sales industry, our founders recognized a critical need for a platform that specifically addresses the unique challenges faced by independent contractors in the healthcare sales space.

What started as a small job board has evolved into a comprehensive ecosystem that provides resources, community, and opportunities for 1099 representatives across the United States.

Our Mission & Values

We're committed to empowering independent medical sales representatives with the tools, resources, and connections they need to thrive.

Community First

We believe in the power of community and connection. Our platform fosters collaboration, mentorship, and knowledge sharing among independent sales professionals.

Continuous Growth

We're dedicated to providing resources that help our members develop their skills, expand their knowledge, and grow their businesses.

Integrity & Trust

We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our interactions, ensuring that both representatives and companies can trust our platform.
