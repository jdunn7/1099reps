About This Calculator

The 1099 REPS Commission Calculator helps independent medical sales representatives estimate their potential earnings based on various factors including sales type, compensation structure, experience level, and territory size.

How It Works

Our calculator uses industry data and average commission rates for different medical sales specialties to provide realistic earnings estimates. The calculation takes into account:

  • Base Salary or Draw: Fixed compensation component (if applicable)
  • Commission Rate: Percentage of sales paid as commission
  • Sales Volume: Projected annual sales in your territory
  • Business Expenses: Estimated costs of operating as a 1099 contractor
  • Tax Considerations: Estimated self-employment and income taxes
