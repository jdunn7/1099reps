Protect Your Business with Professional Contracts

As an independent medical sales representative, having well-drafted contracts is essential for protecting your business interests, clarifying expectations, and establishing professional relationships with manufacturers and healthcare facilities.

Our customizable contract templates are designed specifically for the medical sales industry and have been reviewed by attorneys with expertise in healthcare and independent contractor law.

Pro Tip

While our templates provide a solid foundation, we recommend having your final contracts reviewed by an attorney familiar with your state's laws and the specific requirements of the medical sales industry.

Key Contract Elements for Medical Sales Reps

When customizing your contracts, pay special attention to these critical elements:

  • Commission Structure: Clearly define how commissions are calculated, when they're earned, and payment schedules.
  • Territory Rights: Specify your geographic territory and whether you have exclusive rights within that area.
  • Term and Termination: Include the contract duration and conditions under which either party can terminate the agreement.
  • Post-Termination Commissions: Address whether you'll receive commissions on sales initiated during your contract but finalized after termination.
  • Non-Compete Clauses: Ensure any restrictions are reasonable in scope, geography, and duration.
  • Compliance Requirements: Include provisions regarding adherence to healthcare regulations and industry standards.
